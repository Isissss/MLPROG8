Heart Failure Mortality Chance Checker

This is a machine learning model that predicts the chance of death from heart failure based on the data in the form for people between 40-79.

Disclaimer: This model has an accuracy of 78%. While it may be used as an indicator to improve early detection, a second opinion from a doctor is required.
